Spatioform (Spatial + Form) Lab

We explore new frontiers in understanding the 3D physical world. By integrating machine learning with physical simulation, we develop end-to-end pipelines for scene understanding, digital representation, and advanced engineering applications. Our research leverages Agentic AI to automate complex reasoning, decision-making, and design tasks, enabling intelligent systems that can perceive, analyze, and interact with complex environments. We emphasize scalable algorithms that bridge the gap between fundamental research, rapid prototyping, and transformative real-world impact across engineering, robotics, computer vision, and computer graphics.
