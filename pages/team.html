Spatioform (Spatial + Form) Lab

We explore new frontiers in understanding and synthesizing the 3D physical world. By integrating learning-based methods with high-fidelity physical simulation, we develop end-to-end pipelines for scene understanding and advanced engineering applications. Our research leverages Agentic AI to automate complex reasoning and design tasks, emphasizing scalable algorithms that bridge the gap between rapid prototyping and transformative real-world impact.
